Nitya's Top 3 Favourite Movies

Movies that make you feel good and keep you coming back no matter how many times you've watched them

The Lord of the Rings

Yes, I know these are 3 movies and it's cheating, but it's 1 book, folks!

This is the ultimate marathon watchlist movie to have for when you have a nice long weekend or vacation. It's the ultimate story of the triumph of the good and the simple over evil. You won't regret this.

The Intern

This is the ultimate nice, quirky, fun, 'millennial' watch for a quick feel-good movie you want on a week night.

Godavari

This movie perfectly symbolises that lovely warm, rich hot chocolate on a stormy night. This is the ultimate feel-good without all the fantastical and mythical creatures of LOTR. It's your everyday boy-next-door meets girl-next-door movie, but it feels so soothing and slice of life, you feel like you're on the journey with the main characters.
